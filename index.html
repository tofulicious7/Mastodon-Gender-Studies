Gender Studies on Mastodon

Follow all your favourite Gender Studies researchers

You want to get in touch with Gender Studies researchers from around the world on Mastodon? This page lists accounts from researchers in the field of Gender Studies and institutions and offers an easy method to follow multiple accounts at once. Just decide if you want to follow all or only some of the accounts listed below. You are provided with a .csv-file, which you can upload in Mastodon in order to follow all accounts included. Here you can find a short instruction, on how to do this.

Further information on how to be added or removed can be found at the bottom of this page.

Clicking on a keyword or language allows you to filter the list; clicking the activated keyword/language again will undo the filter.

Accounts by handle, name, and profile link (most recent first)

Full names are only included if they are shown in the profile or on explicit demand.

If you would like to appear on this list, too, please fill out this form or contact us on Mastodon @genderstudiesLSA@hcommons.social or via E-Mail gendercampus@kgc-sachsen-anhalt.de

Based on Sociologists on Mastodon by David Adler, Thomas Haase & Hendrik Erz. If you want to contribute to the main project, please visit this GitHub-Repository.
